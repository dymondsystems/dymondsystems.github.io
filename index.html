About us


We want to make your experience with renovation and repair contractors as seamless as possible by combining all services into a single entity.


Thank you for your interest in Dymond! We offer a multitude of services from handy man work to property management. Dymond systems has proven to be a very different kind of company. As a service-inspired commercial/residential/new construction company with a Team-Driven approach to building, we focus on what is really important: partnering with our clients and working together to meet their unique service goals. Services offered include painting, flooring (tile, hardwood, carpet, laminate, and specialty floor coatings) roofing, pressure washing, plumbing, landscaping, carpentry, electrical, and much more.
This approach enables Dymond to foster a culture of relationship building that allows for not only tremendous satisfaction, but a great deal of repeat business – over 70% of our projects come from existing clients who understand that when they partner with Dymond they will save time and money, and gives them maximum quality and service from a single trusted source.
